Tojo Headquarters

Tojo HQ(a.k.a. Tojo Headquarters) was created in December 8th, 2012. Our objective, since that day, is to bring the best, most enjoyable and most accurate content. Our driving force is the love for the franchise, and what keep this site alive are you: the Yakuza fans!

Welcome to TojoHQ: The Headquarters of the Yakuza fans!

Review Policy

On the ocasion that a game review is made using a copy provided by its publisher, it will be mentioned as a disclaimer. Receiving review copies(or not) in no way affects our veridict about a game.
